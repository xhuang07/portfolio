Dark & Sweet

Project Type
Book Design
Tools
Indesign
Photoshop
Transforming a 'low-brow' themed TV show and its contents into a beautifully bound book, this project pushes the limits of how a person interacts with a book to digest its contents.
The book analyzes each character from two angles: one delves into the hidden 'dark' side, while the other reveals the 'sweet' side. I meticulously adopted the French fold technique to 'hide' one book inside another. On its hardcover, there is a pill-shaped button that can be turned either vertically or horizontally. Depending on the direction the user unconsciously chooses, they open up a book that presents a unique perspective about this show.
