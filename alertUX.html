Overview
To alert or not to alert, that is the question.
Split is a feature delivery platform which enables companies to embrace impact driven development across their engineering teams. Causal alerting is a crucial differentiator for us in the market. As of July 2022, around 95 customers have purchased the Monitor pack, but less than 10% of them are actually using its main capabilities.
In my role as the embedded solo designer within the Measurement and Learning team, I collaborated with the PM and engineering team to investigate the current issues and explore ways to enhance the usability of the alerting feature.
Let’s dive in...not to spoil anything but this story comes with a twist~

How might we Improve usability of our alerting feature
Through discussions with customers, prospects, and internal teams like Sales/CS/Marketing, anecdotal feedback indicated that the perceived value of causal alerting resonates strongly, but a bumpy experience prevents customers from realizing the actual value of our offering.
