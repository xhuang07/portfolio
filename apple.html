Apple of My Eyes

Project Type
Data Visualization
Tools
Javascript
Illustrator
CSV
Do artists randomly choose apples to depict in their artworks, or is there a deeper cultural significance associated with this choice?
Apples appear in many religious traditions, often as a mystical or forbidden fruit. Consequently, in the story of Adam and Eve, the apple became a symbol for knowledge, immortality, temptation, the fall of man into sin, and sin itself. Through collaboration with the Metropolitan Museum, we were granted access to their API, allowing us to retrieve all the artwork’s data.
For more process details, you may visit my github.
