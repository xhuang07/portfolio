Apple of The Met

Project Type
Data Visualization
Tools
Html + Javascript + CSS
Illustrator
CSV
Do artists randomly choose apples to depict in their artworks, or is there a deeper cultural significance associated with this choice?
As an interactive counterpart to 'The Apple of My Eyes,' this interactive data visualization takes audiences on a linear narrative journey. Through this visualization, the audience can examine and appreciate each 'apple' inside the Met Museum, which may typically be overlooked within the frame. Additionally, they can delve into different aspects of apples in the Met collection to see which cultures use apples more frequently than others.
