Apple of The Met

Project Type
Data Visualization
Tools
Javascript
Illustrator
CSV
Do artists just pick apple as a random fruit to depict in their artworks or is it highly associated with cultural significance?
As an interactive counter piece for the Apple of My Eyes, this interactive data visualization provides audiences with a linear narrative journey. Through this visualization audience can look closer and appreciate each ‘apple’ inside Met Museum which usually have been ignored from the frame. Also dive into different aspects of apples inside met collection to see which culture uses apple more frequently than the other.
