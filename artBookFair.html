Flip The Page

Project Type
Visual Identity
Tools
Illustrator
Indesign
Book is the dancing of papers.
This is an identity and overall experience design project for New York Art Book Fair. My inspiration spark came one day to me while I was reading a book, I realized all the movements it involved is actually to flip the page, therefore I picked the theme for this art book fair is ‘paper’. Everything is about ‘paper’ …for signage design, I used paper directly and folded it in the same direction as it’s pointed at. On the ticket I marked what type of paper it’s using, just to add a touch of surprise to the whole identity. I also created a gird which is is composed by two intersecting rectangles, and designed a whole alphabet based on this grid.
