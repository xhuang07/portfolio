Flip The Page

Project Type
Visual Identity
Tools
Illustrator
Indesign
Book is the dancing of papers.
This is an identity and overall experience design project for the New York Art Book Fair. The inspiration struck me one day while reading a book; I realized that all the movements involved in reading are actually about flipping the page. Therefore, I chose the theme for this art book fair to be 'paper.' Everything revolves around 'paper.' For the signage design, I used paper directly and folded it in the same direction as it's pointed at. On the ticket, I indicated the type of paper used, adding a touch of surprise to the overall identity. Additionally, I created a grid composed of two intersecting rectangles and designed a whole alphabet based on this grid.
