A deluxe book commemorates Quinnipiac President John L. Lahey and his transformative leadership of the university.
The book is illustrated with historical images from Quinnipiac’s archive, including large, full-page portraits of key figures at the university. The original photographs varied in quality for reproduction, so we treated the images with a bitmap effect to give them a cohesive look.
