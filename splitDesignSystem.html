What’s Tango?
Once upon a time…Split app doesn’t have any formalized design system. Each team creates their own source of standards for UI elements, components, or modules inside the platform.
As a result we discovered:
  • Conflicting guidelines and direction.
  • Duplication of common components.
  • Inconsistencies across market websites and micro-sites.
  • Pressure for agile solutions without discovery and research.
