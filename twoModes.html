Two Modes Travel App

Project Type
App Concept
Tools
Sketch
Indesign
A duality is a situation in which two opposite things coexist at the same time.
As a counterpart for the physical D&S book, I also designed a mobile application based on the same theme but has different functionality. This app has two modes, the mode will switch with each other automatically based on time. Before 12:00am, it's a normal travel guide app of NYC which recommends food that's been shown in the Gossip Girl TV show. After 12:00am, it shows night club and some secretive places in NYC area.
